Which organelle is responsible for producing energy in the form of ATP through cellular respiration?
Mitochondria

Which organelle processes, modifies, and packages proteins and lipids for transport within and outside the cell?
Golgi apparatus

Which cellular structure is the site of protein synthesis in the cell, where amino acids are assembled into polypeptide chains?
Ribosome

What is the name of the organelle that stores water, nutrients, and waste products, providing turgor pressure in plant cells?
Vacuole

Which organelle is responsible for photosynthesis in plant cells, converting sunlight into chemical energy?
Chloroplast

What is the name of the extensive network of membranes involved in the synthesis of lipids and proteins in the cell?
Endoplasmic reticulum

Which substructure within the nucleus is responsible for the production of ribosomal RNA (rRNA)?
Nucleolus

What is the name of the organelle that plays a crucial role in cell division by organizing microtubules in animal cells?
Centriole

Which small, membrane-bound sac is involved in the transport of various molecules within and outside the cell?
Vesicle

Which plastid, found in plant cells, is responsible for the synthesis and storage of starches and other carbohydrates?
Leucoplast

Which hair-like structures extend from the surface of certain cells, aiding in movement or the movement of substances over the cell's surface?
Cilia

What is the name of the plastid responsible for storing pigments, giving color to fruits, flowers, and some leaves?
Chromoplast

Which organelle contains enzymes responsible for breaking down cellular waste and debris, serving as the cell's "garbage disposal"?
Lysosome

Which semi-fluid substance fills the cell, serving as the medium in which organelles are suspended and where many cellular processes occur?
Cytoplasm

What is the central organelle in eukaryotic cells that contains the cell's genetic material and controls cell activities?
Nucleus
